Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Overview

Sidequest is designed to keep your quest activity on your device. We do not run our own account system, advertising network, or analytics service in the app.

Information the app stores

Sidequest may store your display name, preferences, generated quests, quest history, badges, blocked keywords, notes, and quest photos locally on your device so the app can work.

Photos and camera access

If you choose to add a photo to a completed quest, the photo is used for that quest history entry. Photos remain on your device unless you choose to share them through iOS sharing features.

Purchases and subscriptions

Premium purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. Sidequest may read your App Store purchase status to unlock premium features, but payment details are handled by Apple.

Support email

If you contact support by email, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your email address, device details, and message. We use it only to respond to your request.

Third parties

Sidequest uses Apple services such as the App Store and iOS system features. Those services are governed by Apple's privacy policies. We do not sell your personal information.

Deleting your data

Most app data is stored locally. You can remove it by deleting the app from your device. You can also contact support if you have questions about information sent by email.
